About Afro Jewel

Afro Jewel is dedicated to showcasing the best of Zimbabwe. We specialize in facilitating visas, organizing exciting tours for visitors, and arranging educational trips for schools across Zimbabwe and neighboring regions.

Our team is committed to creating unique and memorable experiences, whether you’re seeking adventure, education, or relaxation. Let us show you the beauty of Zimbabwe with care and expertise.

Our Services

Visa Services

Afro Jewel makes the process of obtaining visas for Zimbabwe hassle-free. Our team guides you through all documentation for smooth entry into Zimbabwe.

Tours for Visitors

Join our professionally guided tours across Zimbabwe, from scenic vistas to rich cultural landmarks and thrilling activities.

School Tours

Educational tours designed for schools, offering engaging experiences at historical, cultural, and ecological sites throughout Zimbabwe.

Our Destinations

Victoria Falls

Witness the awe-inspiring “Smoke That Thunders” at one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World.

Activities:
  • Bungee jumping over the falls
  • White-water rafting
  • Sunset cruises on the Zambezi River

Hwange National Park

Explore Zimbabwe's largest park, home to elephants, lions, and a stunning variety of bird species.

Activities:
  • Wildlife safaris
  • Bird-watching tours
  • Campfire evenings

Matobo Hills

Discover ancient rock formations, sacred hills, and some of the world’s oldest cave paintings.

Activities:
  • Explore ancient rock art
  • Guided spiritual tours
  • Rock climbing

Lake Kariba

Relax at one of the largest man-made lakes, famous for its stunning sunsets and water-based activities.

Activities:
  • Fishing trips
  • Houseboat cruises
  • Waterside camping

Nyanga

Immerse yourself in lush landscapes, waterfalls, and Zimbabwe’s highest peaks in the scenic region of Nyanga.

Activities:
  • Hiking trails
  • Waterfall tours
  • Fishing in Nyangombe River

Heroes Acre

Visit Zimbabwe's national shrine and learn about the historical figures who shaped the nation’s history.

Activities:
  • Guided historical tours
  • Monument visits
  • Educational insights on national heritage

Mana Pools

A UNESCO World Heritage site offering unique walking safaris in pristine wilderness.

Activities:
  • Walking safaris
  • Canoeing experiences
  • Wildlife photography
